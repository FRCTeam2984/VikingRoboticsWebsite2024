OUTREACH

Vikings Robotics is devoted to competition, but that is not our only mission. Our members work year-round to build-up our community through inspiring younger scholars. We participate in outreach programs with local businesses, teach students about real-world skills, and emphasize STEM and STEM education. Vikings Robotics also actively promotes diversity and inclusion, and our team is focused on racial and gender based equality.

Riford Library Workshop

Riford Library Picture

Riford Library Picture

Riford Library Picture

The Vikings Robotics team helped run the lego robotics workshop at Riford library on May 19, 2024. The theme was “At the Beach”, where middle school students learned how to program with EV3 (a type of block coding language for competition LEGO robotics) through a series of challenges related to the beach. These included things like programming the LEGO robot to push trash on the beach into the dumpster area, and pushing a lever to allow the LEGO surfer to surf the wave!
