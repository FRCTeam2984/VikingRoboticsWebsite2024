OUTREACH

Vikings Robotics is devoted to competition, but that is not our only mission. Our members work year-round to build-up our community through inspiring younger scholars. We participate in outreach programs with local businesses, teach students about real-world skills, and emphasize STEM and STEM education. Vikings Robotics also actively promotes diversity and inclusion, and our team is focused on racial and gender based equality.
